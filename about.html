About Me

Hi! I’m Parul Aware, a passionate and driven third-year engineering student pursuing a degree in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science from Marathwada Mitra Mandal’s College of Engineering, Pune. Ever since I stepped into this field, I’ve been fascinated by how technology can solve real-world problems and make lives easier. That curiosity has guided my learning journey and shaped me into someone who loves to explore, build, and create.

Over the years, I’ve developed a strong technical foundation through both academics and personal projects. I specialize in Full Stack Development with skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and I’m also well-versed in Data Structures and Algorithms using C++. I have a basic understanding of Python, which I continue to expand as I explore AI-related tools and libraries. Alongside development, I place high importance on clean documentation, ensuring that the work I do is structured, understandable, and easy to maintain.

But beyond just technical skills, what truly defines me is my collaborative spirit. I’ve learned the importance of teamwork, adapting to new environments, and understanding diverse perspectives. Whether I’m leading a task or contributing to a group project, I always aim to meet deadlines, support others, and keep communication open and respectful.

I genuinely enjoy innovating — whether it’s experimenting with a new idea, designing a user-friendly interface, or brainstorming unique solutions. The process of turning a small concept into something meaningful excites me. I’m always looking for opportunities to grow, challenge myself, and make an impact through technology.

As I continue this journey, I’m eager to work on more real-world projects, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and keep pushing my boundaries — both as a student and as an aspiring technologist.
